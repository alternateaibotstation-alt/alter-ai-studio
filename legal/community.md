# Community Guidelines for ONE-Stop AI Platform

**Last Updated: April 5, 2026**

These Community Guidelines outline the expectations for behavior and content within the ONE-Stop AI Platform (the "Service"), including our forums, marketplaces, and any other interactive areas. These guidelines are an extension of our Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy. By participating in the ONE-Stop community, you agree to abide by these rules.

## 1. Be Respectful and Inclusive

*   **Treat Others with Kindness:** Engage in constructive and civil discussions. Personal attacks, insults, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.
*   **Promote Inclusivity:** Our community is diverse. Do not post content that discriminates against, demeans, or excludes individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, disability, disease, age, sexual orientation, gender, or gender identity.
*   **Respect Privacy:** Do not share personal or private information about others without their explicit consent.

## 2. Share Constructively

*   **Provide Helpful Feedback:** When reviewing templates, bots, or other creations, offer constructive criticism that helps others improve. Focus on the content, not the creator.
*   **Stay On-Topic:** Keep discussions relevant to the ONE-Stop platform, AI, content creation, or related topics.
*   **No Spam or Self-Promotion:** Avoid excessive self-promotion, unsolicited advertising, or spamming the community with irrelevant content.

## 3. Uphold Content Standards

All content you create, upload, or share within the ONE-Stop community must comply with our Content Policy. Specifically, this means:

*   **No Illegal Content:** Do not post or promote anything illegal.
*   **No Harmful or Hateful Content:** This includes hate speech, glorification of violence, or misinformation.
*   **No Sexually Explicit Content:** Keep all content appropriate for a general audience.
*   **Respect Intellectual Property:** Only share content you have the rights to. Do not infringe on copyrights or trademarks.
*   **Transparency with AI:** If you are sharing AI-generated content that could be misleading, clearly label it as such.

## 4. Responsible Use of AI Companions

If you interact with or create AI companions on the platform:

*   **Remember They Are AI:** AI companions are not human. Do not treat them as substitutes for professional help (medical, psychological, legal) or genuine human relationships.
*   **Avoid Harmful Prompts:** Do not intentionally prompt AI companions to generate harmful, abusive, or inappropriate responses.
*   **Report Misbehavior:** If an AI companion exhibits unexpected or harmful behavior, please report it to us.

## 5. Reporting Violations

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates these Community Guidelines, please report it to us immediately at carleylenon@gmail.com. When reporting, please provide as much detail as possible, including links to the content or specific usernames.

## 6. Enforcement

We reserve the right to enforce these guidelines at our sole discretion. Actions we may take include, but are not limited to:

*   Removing violating content.
*   Issuing warnings.
*   Temporarily suspending accounts.
*   Permanently terminating accounts.

We do not have an obligation to monitor all content, but we will investigate reports and take appropriate action.

## 7. Changes to these Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new guidelines on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review these guidelines periodically for any changes.
