## 1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the Service, including:

### 1.1. Personal Information

*   **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password. If you choose to provide it, we may also collect your avatar image.
*   **Payment Information:** If you make purchases or subscribe to our Service, we collect payment information such as your credit card details. This information is processed by third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe) and we do not directly store your full payment card details.
*   **Communication Information:** When you communicate with us (e.g., customer support, feedback), we collect the content of your communications and any contact information you provide.

### 1.2. Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your use of the Service, including:
*   **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
*   **Log Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on the Service, search queries, and interactions with AI models.
*   **AI Interaction Data:** Details of your interactions with AI models, including prompts, inputs, generated content, model used, token counts, and associated costs. This data is used for service improvement, cost optimization, and billing purposes.

### 1.3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (e.g., web beacons, pixels) to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. These technologies are used for:
*   **Authentication:** To keep you logged in.
*   **Preferences:** To remember your settings and preferences.
*   **Analytics:** To understand how users interact with our Service and improve its functionality.
*   **Marketing:** To deliver relevant advertisements.

For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
