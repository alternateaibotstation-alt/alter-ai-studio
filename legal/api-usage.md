ONE-Stop offers a "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) feature, allowing users to integrate their personal API keys from providers like OpenAI or Google Gemini. This feature provides greater control over usage and costs, but also entails specific responsibilities.

*   **Usage Monitoring:** Monitoring your own API usage directly with the respective third-party provider. This includes tracking token consumption, request rates, and any other metrics relevant to your billing and usage limits.
*   **Cost Management:** Managing all costs and billing directly with the third-party API provider. ONE-Stop will not be responsible for any charges, overages, or unexpected expenses incurred through your BYOK usage.

*   **Data Handling:** Understanding and agreeing to how the third-party API provider handles your data, prompts, and generated content, as per their privacy policies.

When you use your BYOK key:

*   **Proxy Service:** ONE-Stop acts as a proxy, forwarding your requests and your API key to the third-party provider. We do not store your full API key on our servers permanently, but may temporarily process it to authenticate requests.
