# Content Policy for ONE-Stop AI Platform

**Last Updated: April 5, 2026**

This Content Policy outlines the guidelines and restrictions for content created, uploaded, or shared on the ONE-Stop AI Platform (the "Service"). This policy is an integral part of our Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy. By using the Service, you agree to adhere to these content standards. Violations may lead to content removal, account suspension, or termination.

## 1. General Principles

Our goal is to foster a creative and safe environment. All content on ONE-Stop should be:

*   **Respectful:** Treat all users and subjects with respect. Avoid content that is hateful, discriminatory, or promotes harm.
*   **Lawful:** Comply with all applicable local, state, national, and international laws and regulations.
*   **Original or Licensed:** Ensure you have the necessary rights or licenses for any content you upload or use as input for AI generation.
*   **Transparent:** Clearly label AI-generated content where required by law or platform guidelines, especially if it could be mistaken for human-created content or news.

## 2. Prohibited Content Categories

The following types of content are strictly prohibited on ONE-Stop:

### 2.1. Illegal Content

*   Content that promotes, facilitates, or depicts illegal activities (e.g., drug manufacturing, illegal weapons, child exploitation).
*   Content that incites violence, terrorism, or criminal behavior.

### 2.2. Harmful and Hateful Content

*   **Hate Speech:** Content that attacks or demeans a group based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, disability, disease, age, sexual orientation, gender, or gender identity.
*   **Harassment and Bullying:** Content intended to intimidate, demean, or harass individuals or groups.
*   **Glorification of Violence:** Content that glorifies or promotes violence, self-harm, or suicide.
*   **Misinformation and Disinformation:** Content that is demonstrably false or misleading and could cause public harm, including but not limited to election interference, public health misinformation, or conspiracy theories.

### 2.3. Sexually Explicit and Obscene Content

*   Pornography or sexually explicit material.
*   Content depicting sexual violence or non-consensual sexual acts.
*   Content involving the sexual exploitation or abuse of children.

### 2.4. Intellectual Property Infringement

*   Content that infringes on copyrights, trademarks, patents, trade secrets, or other proprietary rights of others.
*   Unauthorized use of copyrighted material, including images, text, music, or video, without proper licensing or permission.

### 2.5. Private and Confidential Information

*   Content that reveals personal, private, or confidential information about individuals without their consent (e.g., doxxing, sharing private medical records).

### 2.6. Deceptive and Fraudulent Content

*   Content that is deceptive, fraudulent, or designed to mislead users (e.g., phishing, scams, impersonation).
*   Content that promotes pyramid schemes, multi-level marketing, or other deceptive financial practices.

### 2.7. Malicious Content

*   Content that contains or links to malware, viruses, or other harmful code.

## 3. AI-Specific Content Guidelines

Given the nature of an AI platform, specific considerations apply to AI-generated content:

*   **Responsibility for AI Output:** You are ultimately responsible for all content generated using your account, even if created by AI. Always review and verify AI outputs before use or publication.
*   **Avoiding Harmful AI Generation:** Do not intentionally prompt AI models to generate content that falls into the prohibited categories listed above.
*   **Deepfakes and Synthetic Media:** If creating synthetic media (e.g., deepfakes), you must disclose its artificial nature, especially if it could be misleading or cause harm. Misleading use of synthetic media is prohibited.

## 4. User-Generated Templates

If you create and offer templates on the ONE-Stop marketplace:

*   Your templates must comply with all aspects of this Content Policy.
*   You must ensure that your templates do not infringe on any intellectual property rights.
*   Templates should not promote or facilitate the creation of prohibited content.
