# Acceptable Use Policy for ONE-Stop AI Platform

**Last Updated: April 5, 2026**

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the types of conduct and content that are prohibited when using the ONE-Stop AI Platform (the "Service"). This AUP is incorporated by reference into our Terms of Service. By using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP. Violation of this AUP may result in the suspension or termination of your account and access to the Service.

## 1. Prohibited Content

You agree not to use the Service to create, upload, post, transmit, store, or otherwise make available any content that:

*   **Is Illegal:** Violates any applicable local, state, national, or international law or regulation.
*   **Is Harmful or Threatening:** Promotes violence, terrorism, self-harm, or illegal activities. Includes hate speech, harassment, or bullying.
*   **Is Defamatory or Libelous:** Contains false, misleading, or disparaging statements about others.
*   **Is Obscene or Sexually Explicit:** Contains pornography, sexually explicit material, or promotes sexual exploitation of minors.
*   **Infringes Intellectual Property:** Violates the copyright, trademark, patent, trade secret, or other intellectual property rights of any third party.
*   **Invades Privacy:** Discloses personal, private, or confidential information about others without their express consent.
*   **Is Deceptive or Fraudulent:** Involves phishing, scams, or misrepresentation.
*   **Contains Malware:** Includes viruses, worms, Trojan horses, or any other malicious code designed to harm or disrupt computer systems or data.
*   **Promotes Discrimination:** Advocates discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

You agree not to engage in any of the following activities when using the Service:

*   **Unauthorized Access:** Attempting to gain unauthorized access to the Service, other users' accounts, or computer systems or networks connected to the Service.
*   **Interference:** Interfering with or disrupting the integrity or performance of the Service or its related systems or networks.
*   **Abuse of Resources:** Using the Service in a manner that consumes excessive resources (e.g., CPU, memory, network bandwidth) to the detriment of other users or the Service's stability.
*   **Spamming:** Sending unsolicited or unauthorized advertising, promotional materials, junk mail, spam, chain letters, or any other form of solicitation.
*   **Automated Access:** Using any robot, spider, scraper, or other automated means to access the Service for any purpose without our express written permission.
*   **Misrepresentation:** Impersonating any person or entity, or falsely stating or otherwise misrepresenting your affiliation with a person or entity.
*   **Circumvention of Security:** Attempting to circumvent any security or authentication measures of the Service.
*   **Data Mining:** Collecting or harvesting any personally identifiable information from the Service without explicit consent.
*   **Resale of Service:** Reselling, renting, leasing, or otherwise providing access to the Service to any third party without our express written permission.
*   **Creating Harmful AI:** Using the AI generation capabilities to create or promote AI that is designed to be harmful, deceptive, or to spread misinformation at scale.
