# Alter AI: The AI Monetization Engine for Creators

## Product Positioning

**Alter AI** is not just another AI content generator—it's a **complete monetization engine** that enables creators to generate, monetize, and distribute AI-powered content at scale.

### The Problem We Solve

Creators face three critical challenges:

1. **Content Creation Bottleneck** - Creating quality content manually is time-consuming
2. **Monetization Complexity** - Multiple platforms, affiliate programs, and revenue streams are fragmented
3. **Distribution Friction** - Getting content to the right audience requires manual effort

### Our Solution

Alter AI combines three core capabilities:

1. **AI Content Generation** - Create viral-ready content in 30 seconds
2. **Built-in Monetization** - Affiliate links, referral rewards, and revenue sharing automatically embedded
3. **Viral Distribution** - Watermarked, shareable content that drives traffic and conversions

### 4. Referral Revenue

- **DAU/MAU:** Daily/Monthly active users

- **Content sharing rate:** Target 60%+

### Monetization Metrics
- **ARPU:** Average revenue per user
- **LTV:** Lifetime value per creator
- **Marketplace GMV:** Gross merchandise value
- **Affiliate revenue:** Total affiliate earnings

### Viral Metrics
- **Share rate:** % of content shared
- **CTR:** Click-through rate on affiliate links
- **Conversion rate:** % of clicks → conversions
- **Referral conversion:** % of referrals → signups
